Executive Summary

This project builds a Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) framework to identify high-value customer segments and optimize long-term revenue planning.

Using predictive modeling and RFM-based segmentation, the analysis highlights where acquisition, retention, and reactivation investments generate the strongest business return.

Key Discoveries:

  • Revenue Concentration: Top customer segments drive a major share of total value contribution.
  • Segment Prioritization: Value-based cohorts enable smarter campaign targeting and budget allocation.
  • Retention Leverage: Protecting high-CLV customers delivers stronger margin impact than broad discounts.
  • Projected Impact: CLV-led strategy supports sustained growth through better resource allocation.
